The Venue

Les Maisons Monplaisir

Our wedding celebration will take place at Les Maisons Monplaisir, a beautiful seventeenth-century estate in the Périgord region of South-West France.

Surrounded by countryside, gardens and terraces, the estate includes La Chartreuse and the renovated Old Stables, providing accommodation for our guests throughout the weekend. The estate is composed of 3 buildings, 2 swimming pools, a pool table and loads of other cool things.

Full address, room allocations and arrival information will be shared soon.

Getting There

Travel Information

Guests will need to arrange their own flights. We recommend booking Ryanair flights from Edinburgh to Bergerac once they become available, usually around 6 months in advance and should cost no more than 100pp if booking with Ryanair.

Questions

FAQs

Is accommodation included?

Yes. Accommodation at the property has been paid for.

Do we need to arrange our own flights?

Yes. Guests will need to arrange their own flights. We recommend booking Ryanair flights from Edinburgh to Bergerac once they become available, usually around 6 months in advance.

We will arrange transfers from Bergerac Airport to the property for everyone arriving there.

A few of us may be driving over. If you would prefer to drive, or would like to join others travelling by car, please let us know.

What is the dress code?

Formal attire for the wedding day.

How will food and drinks work?

Closer to the time, we will calculate the cost of pastries for each day, non-alcoholic drinks, alcoholic drinks and snacks for everyone.

Everyone will be asked to contribute, and food and drinks will be distributed across the three buildings accordingly.

When will room allocations be shared?

Room allocations will be shared around one month before arrival.

Everyone will either share with someone they know or stay with their partner.

Can I bring a plus one?

No. There are no plus ones, as the property is strictly limited to 32 people at any given time.

Are pets allowed?

No pets are allowed.

What happens if something is already broken when we arrive?

Please notify us of any broken items within 24 hours of arrival if you notice anything damaged or not working.

What happens if I break or damage something?

We have paid a large security deposit for the property. If anything is broken or damaged during the stay, the person responsible will be liable for the cost.

Is smoking allowed?

No. There is no smoking anywhere on the premises.

Please respect this rule. We would be extremely disappointed if anyone ignored it.
